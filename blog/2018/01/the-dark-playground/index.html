The Dark Playground

Writer for Tim Urban gave a great TED talk on procrastination. He jokingly suggests that we all have an instant gratification monkey in our minds.

When the monkey is in control, we are only after short term feeling good and not long term wins.

I think the instant gratification monkey is most insidious when he pretends to be productive. You think you're getting stuff done, but all you're really doing is shuffling things around looking busy.
